INTRODUCTION					*telop-introduction*

*vim-telop* is a Vim plugin to flow telop

Requirements:
- Vim 7.4 or later

Latest version:
https://github.com/nil2nekoni/vim-telop

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMMANDS					*telop-commands*

						*:TelopFlow*
:TelopFlow {message}
	Flow {message} from right to left
>
	" Flow "foo"
	:TelopFlow foo

	" Flow "Hello World"
	:TelopFlow Hello World
<
